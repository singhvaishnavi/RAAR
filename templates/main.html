About Us


ZAAR (Zomato Analysis and Recommendations) is a prediction, recommendations and analysis system for new business seekers in the field of Restaurant Chain in Bangalore. We focus on analysing the reviews of customers for restaurants on Zomato and predicting the supposed ratings that restaurant will get based upon the values in the fields that you will enter. This rating can be used by you to predict the success score of how likely the business would succeed of a newly opened restaurant, this will allow you to choose better practices to gain a better customer base. Along with the predicted rating, an analysis of the result will also shown to you so that the you can see how the fields that they entered will affect the predicted rating. There will be visualizations which will be rendered in the visualizations page which will is a visual insight for the users to determine the areas which affect the overall predicted rating and suggestions for the fields that you can change in order to get a better rating.
